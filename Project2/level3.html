Everything





in      slow             motion
I calculate the time
required
to move
to a
place
I    dont    want    to    be
yet
I dont want to stay
where I am
my feet drag
along
the
ground
kicking
dust in the air
dust
that chokes me
it fills my breath
begins to cake
in my eyes
a dark brown mud
that would blot out the light
if there was any
