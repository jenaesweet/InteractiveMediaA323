Despair Level 3.3

    Everything in slow motion





    I calculate the time required





    To move to a place





    I don't want to be





    Yet I don't want to stay





    Where I am





    My feet drag





    along the ground





    Kicking





    Dust in the air





    Dust that chokes me.





    It fills my breath





    Begins to cake in my eyes





    A dark brown mud





    That would blot out the light





    if there was any
