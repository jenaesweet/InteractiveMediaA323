Despair Level 4
    everything in slow motion




    i calculate




    the time required




    to move to a place




    i don't want to be




    yet I don't want to stay




    where I am




    my feet drag




    along the ground




    kicking




    dust in the air




    dust that chokes me.




    it fills my breath




    begins to cake in my eyes




    a dark brown mud




    that would blot out the light




    if there was any
